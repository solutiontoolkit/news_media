## ✨ Key Features

### 📰 News & Publishing — Built for Google Visibility
- **SEO-ready post head** — every article ships with a compelling headline, excerpt, featured image, and search-friendly metadata so stories are discoverable on Google and social search.
- **Featured Stories carousel** with live updates on the homepage.
- **Trending & category system** across a growing library (300+ stories and counting).
- Rich publishing flow: title, category, featured image (URL or direct upload via Firebase Storage), and excerpt for cards and social shares.

### 💰 Creator Earnings System
- Publishers earn from the very first view — transparent rate card, no hidden rules.
- Live **Publisher Dashboard**: total views, total earnings, amount paid out, and current balance at a glance.
- Low barrier to cash out — minimum payout threshold of just 1,000 views.

### 🪪 Signature Cards
- Every publisher gets a branded **Signature Card** — a digital identity card that builds credibility with readers and clients.

### 💼 Jobs Board
- Employers and businesses **post jobs** in minutes.
- Job seekers **browse and apply directly** on-platform — no middlemen.
- Core mission: connect people to real employment opportunities.

### 🎥 Video Tools
- **Video Analyser** — transcribe any video to text with AI.
- **YouTube integration** — connect your channel to auto-upload videos straight from the platform.

### 📱 App-Like Experience
- Installable **PWA** — "Install Southern Report" right from the browser.
- Push notifications for breaking news and activity.
- Fully responsive: native-feeling experience on mobile, tablet, and desktop.

## 💸 How Publisher Earnings Work

1. **Create an account** and open your Publisher Dashboard.
2. **Publish a post** — article or video — with a strong headline and featured image.
3. **Earn ₦200 for every 1,000 views** your content receives.
4. **Track everything live** — views, earnings, and payout history.
5. **Get paid** once you cross the 1,000-view minimum payout threshold.

> Transparent rates. Real payouts. Your audience becomes your income.
